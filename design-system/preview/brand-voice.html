▦ Do
  • Second person. "You hand over spare bottles."
  • Place the geography. "a soft grey ceiling sits over Taygetos."
  • Verdicts, not metrics. "a good day's work."
  • Em dashes, real — never --.
  • Currency prefixed €, minus is Unicode −€60.
▦ Don't
  • No emoji. Ever.
  • No "epic / amazing / unlock / level up".
  • No first person — never "I" or "we".
  • No exclamation marks in narration.
  • No telegraphic UI fragments — write whole sentences.
