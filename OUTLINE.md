# OUTLINE — Outdoor Guide Life *or no Life*

A general skeleton to navigate by. Each phase is a slot to deepen later, one at a time.
The full reasoning behind this lives in `PHASE-ARCHITECTURE.md`; this is the map.

---

## The spine

One continuous **life** across six phases. Each phase pulls you one rung further from the
trail — "*or no Life*" is the cost of climbing. **Winning = what you became**, not a cash number.
Roughly 2–3 seasons per phase; a 15-year-plus career.

## Principles (hold across every phase)

1. **Layer, don't replace** — nothing you could do before disappears; it recedes into optional/automated.
2. **Unit of play = a concrete human situation under a clock** — never a dashboard.

---

## The six phases (to develop one at a time)

### Phase 1 — Solo guide · *adds: GUIDE*
- **Essence:** you *are* the guide — learn the craft, earn certs, master 3+ activities.
- **Recedes:** — (this is the floor)
- **→ to develop:** how each activity unlocks; the cert path; season-to-season growth.

### Phase 2 — Mentor · *adds: DEVELOP PEOPLE*
- **Essence:** take guides under you and shape them; they become named people.
- **Recedes:** every day being yours.
- **→ to develop:** mentoring mechanics; guide identities; the trigger to incorporate.

### Phase 3 — Company / product · *adds: PRODUCTIZE & SELL*
- **Essence:** build packaged programs, sell to travel agents, list them online.
- **Recedes:** ad-hoc bookings; you now guide only by choice.
- **→ to develop:** product design; the B2B sales loop; scheduling guides onto products.

### Phase 4 — Brand / multi-base · *adds: EXPAND & BRAND*
- **Essence:** multiple bases across Greece, a recognized name; your mentees run them.
- **Recedes:** hands-on running of any single base.
- **→ to develop:** the base/region system; manager arcs; brand reputation.

### Phase 5 — Tour operator · *adds: ORCHESTRATE TRIPS*
- **Essence:** design and run multi-day itineraries; survive the logistics cascades.
- **Recedes:** single operations (auto-run, surface only as exceptions).
- **→ to develop:** the itinerary builder; the ferry/transfer crisis loop. *(ship light first)*

### Phase 6 — Regional travel-management company · *adds: STRATEGY*
- **Essence:** run the region as a portfolio — meetings, offers, dilemmas; systemic shocks.
- **Recedes:** everything operational.
- **→ to develop:** the decision/dilemma set; systemic shocks; the final reckoning. *(ship light first)*

---

## Through-lines (ride all six phases)

Reputation · your named guides · the catastrophe heartbeat · the trail-vs-desk choice.

## Endings — *what you became*

The Institution · The Guide Who Stayed · Balance · (+ failure ends: burnout, bankruptcy, sold out, ruined).

---

## Suggested development order

Deepen **1 → 2 → 3 → 4** in sequence — they evolve one engine, so each builds on the last.
Treat **5 and 6** as light narrative layers first; turn them into full sims only if the game earns it.
Everything here is **engine-agnostic** — design now, and decide the TS + Phaser migration before building 5 and 6.
